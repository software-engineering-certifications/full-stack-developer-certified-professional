FULL STACK DEVELOPER CERTIFIED PROFESSIONAL FAQs


• What exactly does a full stack developer do?

A Full Stack Developer is someone who works with the Back End — or server side — of the application as well as the Front End, or client side. Full Stack Developers have to have some skills in a wide variety of coding niches, from databases to graphic design and UI/UX management in order to do their job well.

• How long does it take to become a professional full stack developer?

It depends on your work ethic, you can typically become a full stack developer in 3 – 6 months with dedicated studies. Keep in mind that your learning process does not end after this period of initial training. Because the software world evolves, you must continually keep up with this development.

• Is full stack developer in demand?

According to Bureau of Labor Statistics data, the role of “web developer” is expected to grow 13 percent from 2018–2028, much faster than the average occupation. And in LinkedIn's 2020 Emerging Jobs Report, “full-stack engineer” is number 4 on their list of top emerging jobs for 2020.

• What is the salary of a full stack developer?

Full Stack Developer salary in India ranges between ₹ 2.4 Lakhs to ₹ 19.1 Lakhs with an average annual salary of ₹ 6.7 Lakhs. Salary estimates are based on 9.4k salaries received from Full Stack Developers.

• Why do companies hire full stack developers?

The main reason is that companies hire young developers based on their potential rather than deep domain knowledge. Companies like to teach young developers what they want them to know. As a full stack developer, you have a broad set of technology skills that the company can help you expand further.

• Can a full-stack developer become a data scientist?

Yes it's great. But only great people can build great things. If you think you are above average and smarter than most of your peers this thing will suit you provided you learn with full interest and passion. The journey is long but rewarding.

• Which language is best for full stack developer?

Currently, the most popular languages for full stack developers are Node. js, Python, C#. ASP.NET, and PHP. It's believed that the most common languages for full stack developers in future will be HTML/CSS, and some of the newer ones like AngularJS, Node.

• Which full stack developer is best?

If you are primarily a JavaScript developer, then React JS should be your first choice for front-end development. If you are looking for a recommendation, The React Complete Guide by Max is the best course to start with.

• Can a full stack developer become a software engineer?

You can take a course in full-stack development to get the required knowledge. To become a software engineer, you should have at least a degree in Computer Science.

• Can I become a full stack developer without a degree?

You don't need a CS degree to get a Full Stack Developer job. Full Stack Development is currently the most sought after careers in the IT field. Anyone from any background can prepare themselves to become a Full Stack Developer.
